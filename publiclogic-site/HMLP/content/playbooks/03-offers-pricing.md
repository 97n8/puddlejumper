# Offers and Pricing Framework

## Offer Ladder
1. Listening + Compliance Baseline
   - Stakeholder interviews
   - Workflow and records map
   - Compliance gaps and priority list
   - Output: Baseline report and 90-day plan

2. VAULT: Continuity and Records Package
   - Retention schedule alignment
   - Plain-language process documentation
   - Handoff and continuity binder
   - Output: VAULT package and training

3. ARCHIEVE: Workflow and Governance System
   - End-to-end workflow redesign
   - Board and public-facing governance workflows
   - Output: ARCHIEVE package and governance playbook

4. Pilot Engagement
   - 6-12 week focused scope
   - Target one function or department
   - Output: Documented, compliant workflow + continuity handoff

5. Ongoing Compliance Stewardship
   - Quarterly audits
   - Update documentation as laws, staffing, and practices evolve
   - Output: Updated compliance and continuity documentation

## Packaging
- Pilot (fixed scope)
- Foundational package (VAULT or ARCHIEVE)
- Full engagement (baseline + VAULT + ARCHIEVE)
- Retainer (ongoing stewardship)

## Pricing Model (Template)
- Pilot: $[X] fixed fee
- VAULT/ARCHIEVE package: $[X] fixed fee based on scope
- Retainer: $[X]/month with defined deliverables

## Scope Guardrails
- Works with municipal staff; does not take over operations.
- No software resale or platform lock-in.
- Deliverables are documented workflows, compliance mapping, and continuity packs.

## Required Inputs from Towns
- Point person and decision maker
- Access to current policies and records schedules
- Availability for interviews and review sessions
