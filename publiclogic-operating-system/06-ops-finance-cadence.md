# Operations and Finance Cadence

## Operating Rhythm
- Weekly: delivery standup + pipeline check
- Monthly: financial review and capacity planning
- Quarterly: strategy review, pricing review, and process updates

## Core Internal Roles (Adjust)
- Principal / Lead Consultant
- Operations Lead
- Research and Documentation Lead
- Engagement Manager

## Standard Operating Meetings
- Weekly Delivery Review (45 minutes)
  - Active engagements status
  - Risks and blockers
  - Next-week priorities

- Monthly Business Review (60 minutes)
  - Revenue, cash flow, pipeline
  - Delivery quality and client feedback
  - Hiring or subcontracting needs

- Quarterly Strategy Review (90 minutes)
  - Mission alignment
  - Offer and pricing updates
  - Market feedback
  - Annual plan progress

## Financial Checklist
- Track by engagement: revenue, labor hours, margin
- Maintain cash runway target: [X] months
- Standardize contracts and invoicing cadence
- Budget for travel and municipal event participation
