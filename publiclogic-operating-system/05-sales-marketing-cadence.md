# Sales and Marketing Cadence

## Messaging Pillars
- Compliance and public trust
- Continuity across turnover
- Process-first, not tool-first
- Enablement, not takeover
- Massachusetts municipal context

## Pipeline Stages
1. Awareness: inquiry or referral
2. Discovery: listening call + context intake
3. Baseline: compliance map proposal
4. Pilot: scoped engagement proposal
5. Active: delivery
6. Closeout: continuity handoff
7. Stewardship: quarterly compliance checks

## Weekly Cadence
- 2 outreach touchpoints to target towns or referrals
- 1 listening call
- 1 pipeline review (30 minutes)

## Monthly Cadence
- Publish one short insight or case note
- Attend or support a MA municipal forum
- Review win/loss notes

## Collateral Checklist
- One-page overview
- Pilot scope template
- Case study template
- Continuity package sample

## Discovery Call Script (Outline)
- What compliance or continuity risks keep you up at night?
- Where do workflows break when staff changes?
- Which records or processes are hardest to manage?
- What would success look like in 90 days?
