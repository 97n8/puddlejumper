# Swanzey Voting VAULT — 11 Years of Ballots, Elections, Patterns & Forecast

A VAULT workbook built from the **real record**: every Town of Swanzey official-ballot town meeting and
election from **2015–2026**, parsed from the Town's own annual reports (plus 2026 from local news),
distilled into the patterns — and a forecast.

## What it answers

> *What have they done, the patterns, everything — plus where it's heading.*

- **Budget:** real operating + default budgets every year (2015–2025), the +6–7%/yr escalation, and the
  collapse of the default-budget lever.
- **Ballots:** what passes in landslides (credits, reserves) vs. what dies at the 3/5 wall (facility
  bonds), with margins.
- **Elections:** selectman races and the rising turnover/volatility.
- **Governance:** the 3→5 board expansion, the SB2-repeal defeat, the polling-place vote.
- **Pains:** ten documented, ranked — led by administrator churn (3 in ~2 years).
- **Forecast:** budget to 2030, eroding budget margins, the capital mandate, and the execution risk.
