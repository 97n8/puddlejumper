# Swanzey Town Administrator — Interview Kit

The spoken layer that turns the Swanzey analysis into interview readiness. Built to close the gap the
prep-grade flagged: rehearsal, self-positioning, tone, and breadth.

## Contents
1. How to use it + the tone that wins (homework, not critique)
2. Your 90-second open
3. The "why you" bridge (no-experience / will-you-stay / why-you-over-others)
4. Anticipated Swanzey questions with tight answers (facility, revaluation, water, 5-member board, RTK, Avanru, budget, labor, welfare)
5. Four STAR stories (scaffold with your specifics)
6. First 100 days in Swanzey
7. The numbers to have cold (+ the live unknowns to refresh first)
8. Breadth quick-hits (procurement, records, roads, emergency mgmt, CIP, personnel, bonding)
9. Traps to avoid + a closing statement
