## v0.1 — 2026-06-04 (working draft)
- Initial ~11-page strategic review for the Swanzey Board of Selectmen.
- **Thesis:** the town leads with *restraint*; restraint built deep voter trust for a decade and is now
  starving capital and eroding that trust. Fix = upgrade the instrument, keep the value.
- Structure: 10 sections — purpose, executive summary, credit-first, the pattern, the hidden cost, the
  inflection, earning the right to change (incl. the "what we'd do differently & why" table), earning
  the right to spend, a six-tool practical toolkit, and a 12-month landing plan.
- 8 charts (budget trajectory, support erosion, reserves-vs-bonds, default gap, tax components,
  funding stack, tax glide, confidence-and-capital thesis chart).
- Built from the corrected, meeting-year-keyed dataset (see `../swanzey-financial-review/`).
- Rendered to **PDF** (print) and **DOCX** (editable) from a single content model.
- **Status:** draft for discussion — intended to be cut back to what the board judges realistic.
