# 🏗️ Fixing Swanzey — Without a Tax Spike

## A 10-year plan to fund **every** big project. **No new bonds. No 3/5 votes.**

---

### The five things our town needs to fix

🏢 Public Works Facility  ·  🌉 Christian Hill Road Bridge  ·  🛣️ Lower Winchester Street
💧 West Swanzey Water System  ·  ♿ Town Hall Accessibility

### Together: about **$5.3 million**

---

## How we pay for it

| | |
|---|---|
| 💰 **62% — someone else's money** | State bridge aid (80% of the bridge), a state water loan repaid by water rates, and grants |
| 🏦 **38% — our share, saved up** | Simple-majority savings funds (capital reserves) — **not bonds** |
| ❌ **$0 in new bonds** | No 3/5 supermajority votes to lose |

> Last year's $1.2M facility bond got a **majority** (678–485) but needed **3/5**. This plan uses
> **savings funds that need only a simple majority** — so we just build it, no bond required.

---

## What it costs you — on a $300,000 home

| 2027 | 2028 | 2029 | 2030 | 2031 | **2032 →** |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| +$36 | +$66 | +$66 | **+$75** | +$60 | **−$18** |

### A tank of oil a year — briefly. Then your capital costs are **lower than today**, with everything **built.**

---

## Why it's safe

✅ Each fund is a **simple-majority** vote — easy to start, easy to pause
✅ Bridge money is **contingent on state aid** — no aid, no town cost
✅ Water is paid by **water rates**, not your property tax
✅ A **published plan** — no more surprise capital bills
✅ Miss a year? The plan **slows; it never dies**

---

## What we're asking

Approve the **capital reserve funds** and the **grant/aid authorizations** on the warrant.
**Year-one deposits: ~$520,000 — unlocking millions in outside funding.**
